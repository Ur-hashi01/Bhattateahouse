about our

tea house

Welcome to Bhatta Tea House, an embodiment of the rich and aromatic heritage of Assam nestled in the heart of Basistha Chariali, Guwahati. As the mist-kissed lands of Assam cradle the finest tea leaves, we at Bhatta Tea House embark on a journey to bring you an unparalleled tea experience.

Our story begins with a passion for the perfect cup of tea, a beverage that transcends boundaries, cultures, and time itself. Inspired by the lush tea estates and the vibrant tapestry of Assamese traditions, we have curated a collection that celebrates the artistry of tea-making.

At Bhatta Tea House, we take pride in our roots, situated in the picturesque landscapes of Assam, the jewel in India's tea crown. Our commitment to authenticity is unwavering, as we source only the finest tea leaves from the verdant hills, ensuring every sip reflects the essence of Assam's terroir.

Bhatta Tea House is not just a tea stall; it's a sensory journey that transcends the ordinary. We believe in the power of tea to bring people together, fostering connections and creating moments of joy. Our online platform extends the charm of Bhatta Tea House to tea enthusiasts worldwide, inviting you to experience the magic of Assam's tea culture from the comfort of your home.

Join us in savoring the soulful brews that echo the spirit of Assam. Bhatta Tea House is not just a destination for tea; it's a celebration of tradition, flavor, and the timeless joy that a good cup of tea brings. Step into our world, and let the aroma of Assam envelop you in a tea experience like no other.
